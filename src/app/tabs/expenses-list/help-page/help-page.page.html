Help Viewing Expenses
  • Expenses are shown in order of date ascending
  • Tap an expense to view its full details (image with timestamp, title, amount, date, type, notes)
  • The total expense amount is shown at the top of the page
  • Current filters applied are also shown at the top of the page
 Applying Filters
  • On the 'View Expenses' Page, tap 'FILTER' on the top right of the toolbar
  • Set dates for the reqired filter (from and to inclusive) and the expense type
  • Tap 'Apply Filters'
  • Current filters applied are shown at the top of the 'View Expenses' page.
  • The total expense amount, taking in to account the applied filters, is now shown at the top of the page
  • To clear filters, tap 'CLEAR' at the top of the page
  • Before applying a new filter, current filters must be cleared
 Editing/Deleting an Expense
  • Tap on the expense to be edited/deleted
  • To delete, tap the trash icon (top riht of the toolbar)
  • To edit, tap Edit, modify details, and tap 'Submit'
 Adding a New Expense
  • Tap on the 'Add Expense' tab at the bottom of the screen
  • Tap 'Add Expense'
  • Fill in the details for the expense (and take a photo of the receipt), then tap 'Submit'
